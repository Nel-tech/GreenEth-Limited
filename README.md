## 📌 Overview
 
GreenEth Intelligence Network is a digital platform for an agriculture and agro-allied services company operating across crop and livestock production, value-chain activities, consultancy and training, and the import & export of agricultural products in Nigeria.
 
The platform includes a modern landing page for the business and a planned AI-powered farmer advisory system that delivers personalized farm intelligence — what to plant, when to plant, market prices, buyer contacts, weather forecasts, and more — directly to farmers via WhatsApp.
 
---
 
## ✨ Features
 
### Landing Page
- Hero section with brand statement and CTAs
- About Us — company history, mission, and values
- Services breakdown across all business verticals
- Products catalog showcasing available produce and agro-allied goods
- Contact and inquiry form with WhatsApp integration
### AI Advisory System *(coming soon)*
- Farmers submit their details via a simple form
- AI generates a personalized farm advisory report covering:
  - Crop and planting recommendations by state and season
  - Real-time commodity market prices
  - Buyer and off-taker contacts
  - Weather forecast for their location
  - Pest and disease alerts with control methods
  - Transporter and feed mill directory
  - Government loan schemes and policy demystification
- Advisory delivered instantly via WhatsApp
